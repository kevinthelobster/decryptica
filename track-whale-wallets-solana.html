How to Track Whale Wallets on Solana in 2026

One of the most profitable strategies in crypto is following "smart money" - the wallets of successful investors who consistently make profitable trades. In this guide, we'll show you how to track whale wallets on Solana and use this information to improve your trading decisions.

Key Insight: Whale wallets often accumulate tokens before major pumps. Following their activity can give you a significant advantage.

What is Whale Tracking?

Whale tracking involves monitoring the on-chain activity of large crypto holders (whales). These wallets often belong to:

  • Early investors in successful projects
  • Institutional traders
  • Experienced DeFi users
  • Insider investors

When these wallets make moves, it often signals significant market activity.

Best Tools for Whale Tracking

Solscan

Solana's premier block explorer. View any wallet's transaction history, token holdings, and more.

Cost: Free

Portfolio tracker with whale wallet following features.

Another excellent explorer with wallet tagging features.

How to Find Whale Wallets

Method 1: Look at Token Holders

  1. Find a token on Solscan
  2. Click "Holders" tab
  3. Look for wallets with large holdings
  4. Check their transaction history

Method 2: Follow DEX Activity

  1. Monitor Raydium or Orca for large swaps
  2. Note wallet addresses making big trades
  3. Track these wallets over time

Method 3: Use Whale Alert Services

  • Twitter accounts that track large transactions
  • Telegram groups for whale alerts
  • Dedicated services like Bubblemaps

Setting Up Alerts

Don't check manually - set up alerts for whale activity:

  • Twitter: Follow @solscan and whale tracking accounts
  • Telegram: Join whale alert channels
  • Custom: Use blockchain APIs to build custom alerts

Risks of Whale Following

⚠️ Important: Whale following isn't foolproof. Reasons why:
  • Whales can be wrong
  • By the time you see it, it's already priced in
  • Some whales are dumpers, not traders
  • Whales may have better entry points

Our Recommended Strategy

  1. Identify: Find 5-10 consistently profitable whale wallets
  2. Monitor: Track their token purchases
  3. Research: Understand why they're buying
  4. Verify: Wait for confirmation (don't just copy)
  5. Copy: Enter with your own position size
  6. Exit: Follow their exit signals or set stops

Conclusion

Whale tracking is a powerful tool for Solana traders. While it won't make you money instantly, it provides valuable insights into market movements. Combine whale tracking with your own research for the best results.
