## SEO Strategy

### Keywords to Target (High Volume)
1. "best [product] 2026"
2. "[topic] explained"
3. "how to [action]"
4. "[tool] vs [tool]"
5. "[topic] for beginners"

### Content Templates

**Comparison Article:**
```
Title: [Product A] vs [Product B] - Which is Better in 2026?
- Intro (what they are)
- Quick comparison table
- Product A deep dive
- Product B deep dive  
- Pros & Cons each
- Our recommendation
- Related articles
```

**Tutorial:**
```
Title: How to [Achieve Result] in 2026 - Complete Guide
- Why this matters (hook)
- Prerequisites
- Step 1
- Step 2
- Step 3
- Common mistakes to avoid
- Next steps
```

**Listicle:**
```
Title: 10 Best [Category] Tools in 2026
- Quick intro
- #1 [Product] - Best overall
- #2 [Product] - Best for X
- #3 [Product] - Best value
- ...through #10
- Conclusion
```
