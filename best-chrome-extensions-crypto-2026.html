Best Chrome Extensions for Crypto in 2026

Chrome extensions can supercharge your crypto trading. From price alerts to portfolio tracking, these tools make crypto easier. Here are the best extensions every crypto trader should have.

Quick Summary: These extensions will help you track prices, manage portfolios, and trade more efficiently.

1. MetaMask

Purpose: Crypto wallet & DeFi gateway

Essential for interacting with Ethereum and Solana dApps. Store, send, and swap tokens directly from your browser.

2. Phantom Wallet

Purpose: Solana wallet

The most popular Solana wallet. Great for NFT trading and Solana DeFi.

3. CoinGecko/CMC Price Tracker

Purpose: Price tracking

Track prices of thousands of cryptos directly in your browser. Real-time alerts.

4. Glassnode Alerts

Purpose: On-chain alerts

Get alerts when whale wallets move, when exchanges see inflows/outflows, and more.

5. Revoke.cash

Purpose: Security

Check and revoke token approvals. Essential for staying safe in DeFi.

6. DeFi Saver

Purpose: DeFi management

Manage your DeFi positions, automate CDPs, and optimize gas fees.

Bonus Extensions

  • Jupiter Aggregator - Best DEX aggregator
  • Solscan - Block explorer
  • Rabby - Multi-chain wallet

Security Tips

⚠️ Always verify extensions before installing:
  • Check developer info
  • Read reviews
  • Only install from Chrome Web Store
  • Keep extensions updated

Conclusion

These extensions will significantly improve your crypto trading experience. Start with MetaMask and Phantom for wallets, then add tracking and security tools as needed.
