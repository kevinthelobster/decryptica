# Decryptica Marketing Plan

## Objective
Build Decryptica into a high-trust crypto, AI, and automation publication that grows qualified organic traffic, email subscribers, and monetizable intent over the next 90 days.

## 90-Day Goals

| Goal | Target |
|------|--------|
| Organic traffic | 2,000 monthly visits |
| Published articles | 90+ total |
| Email subscribers | 250+ |
| Priority keyword rankings | 10 terms in top 30 |
| Affiliate / commercial pages | 15+ live pages |

## Core Positioning
Decryptica helps readers understand and use crypto, AI, and automation tools with practical, search-driven content that is clear, current, and action-oriented.

### Brand Promise
- Explain complex topics simply
- Publish timely updates with useful context
- Help readers choose tools with confidence
- Turn search traffic into repeat audience and buyer intent

## Audience Segments

### 1. Beginners entering crypto or AI
Needs:
- Plain-English explanations
- Safe setup guides
- Tool recommendations

### 2. Active tool users
Needs:
- Comparisons
- Workflow tutorials
- Product updates and feature coverage

### 3. Commercial-intent searchers
Needs:
- Best-of lists
- Product reviews
- Alternatives and versus pages

## Growth Strategy

### 1. SEO-Led Content Engine
Primary acquisition channel: organic search.

Focus areas:
- Reviews and comparisons for monetizable intent
- Tutorials for authority and long-tail traffic
- News for freshness and publishing velocity
- Listicles and roundups for broad keyword capture

Priority keyword formats:
- `best [tool/category] 2026`
- `[tool] vs [tool]`
- `how to [action]`
- `[topic] explained`
- `[tool] review`
- `[tool] alternatives`

### 2. Conversion Layer
Every article should move readers to a next step:
- Email subscribe CTA
- Related article links
- Category / cluster navigation
- Product comparison or review CTA on informational pages

### 3. Authority Compounding
- Publish consistently enough to build topical depth
- Interlink every article into a defined cluster
- Refresh winners and near-winners monthly
- Build pillar and subpillar coverage around crypto, AI, and automation

## Channel Plan

### Organic Search
Primary engine for traffic growth.

Actions:
- Publish daily search-targeted articles
- Create comparison, review, and tutorial clusters
- Maintain sitemap freshness
- Improve CTR with stronger titles and meta descriptions
- Add FAQ and schema where relevant

### Email
Primary retention channel.

Actions:
- Offer newsletter signup sitewide and in-article
- Create a simple value proposition: practical crypto, AI, and automation updates
- Send weekly digest of best new posts
- Feature trending tools, tutorials, and roundups

### Social Distribution
Support channel, not the main dependency.

Actions:
- Share every new article on X, LinkedIn, and relevant communities
- Repurpose articles into short threads, carousels, or summary posts
- Prioritize reviews, comparisons, and breaking-news takes for distribution

### Partnerships and Backlinks
Secondary authority channel.

Actions:
- Reach out to tool founders and operators after reviews go live
- Pitch roundup inclusion exchanges where credible
- Build linkable assets from guides and data-backed summaries
- Target guest posts or expert quotes in adjacent niches

## Content Mix

| Content Type | Share | Purpose |
|-------------|-------|---------|
| Reviews & Comparisons | 40% | Commercial intent, affiliate potential |
| Tutorials & How-Tos | 25% | Authority and long-tail traffic |
| News & Updates | 20% | Freshness and publishing cadence |
| Lists & Roundups | 15% | Broad keyword capture and clicks |

## Publishing Plan

### Month 1: Foundation
Goals:
- Establish cadence
- Ship core templates consistently
- Build initial topic clusters

Execution:
- Publish 1 article per day minimum
- Prioritize easy-win long-tail queries
- Launch 3 to 5 pillar-supporting tutorials
- Publish at least 4 high-intent comparison or review pages

### Month 2: Growth
Goals:
- Increase output and internal linking density
- Double down on categories showing impressions

Execution:
- Increase to 2 posts per day where possible
- Expand comparison and alternatives coverage
- Refresh top-performing month 1 pages
- Start systematic backlink outreach

### Month 3: Scale
Goals:
- Push winning clusters toward rankings
- Improve conversion and monetization

Execution:
- Maintain 2 to 3 posts per day if quality gates hold
- Add more best-of and buyer-intent pages
- Optimize top landing pages for subscribe and affiliate clicks
- Consolidate or prune underperformers if needed

## Conversion Strategy

### Primary CTAs
- Subscribe for weekly updates
- Read the next article in the cluster
- Compare tools before choosing

### Secondary CTAs
- Visit category pages
- Explore product reviews
- Read beginner guides

### Article-Level Conversion Rules
- Add an email CTA after the intro or first major section
- Include related reads before the conclusion
- Route informational intent to commercial pages where relevant
- Keep CTAs useful, not aggressive
